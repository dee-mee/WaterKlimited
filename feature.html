Years of Experience

We have over 10 years of drilling experience working for both individual and corporate clients. We have the equipment, expertise and experience to handle both shallow and deep wells.

We have a qualified, motivated and professional staff that dedicates itself to ensure you have adequate, clean and safe water.

Satisfied Customers

Our Solutions

As a company in the water sector our services cover all the necessary borehole solutions and aim to deliver as per the customer's requirements. We cover a wide range of services from ground water investigation, abstraction, equipping, test pumping and development of new boreholes, servicing and maintenance of existing wells, erection of water towers, and storage facilities to solar installation as an alternative power source.

We aspire to be your partner accompanying you through your journey to have your own water source, advising you when you encounter challenges, servicing and maintaining your borehole to ensure optimal performance.

Have Your Own Well

You don't have to rely on unreliable water sources or put up with frequent water rationing, you should consider drilling your own borehole. Whether you need it for domestic, for agricultural or for commercial use we will advise on the necessary steps to take and drill the borehole for you.
