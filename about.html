WHO WE ARE

We are a private water drilling company registered in Kenya in 2010 under the company's Act.

We are licensed by the Ministry of Water as a qualified water resource contractor and also licensed by the National Construction Authority.

We offer professional services that include hydro-geological surveys, borehole drilling, pump installation, test pumping, borehole cleaning & rehabilitation, installation of solar panel and construction of water related structures.
