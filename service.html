    About Water Leaders (K) Limited

    Your Trusted Water Solutions Partner

    We are a private water drilling company registered in Kenya in 2010 under the company's Act (Cap 486). We are licensed by the Ministry of Water as a qualified water resource contractor under the water act licence. We are also licensed by the National Construction Authority.

    Professional Team
    Our experienced team ensures quality service delivery on every project.
    Licensed & Certified

    Our Services

    Professional Water Drilling Solutions

    Hydro-geological Survey

    Comprehensive ground water assessment and site evaluation to determine optimal borehole locations and water potential.

    Borehole Drilling

    Professional borehole drilling services using modern equipment to access reliable groundwater sources for residential, commercial, and agricultural needs.

    Test Pumping

    Professional test pumping services to determine borehole yield, water quality, and sustainable abstraction rates.

    Pump Installation

    Professional installation and maintenance of water pumps and pumping systems for efficient water extraction and distribution.

    Steel Tower & Tank Fabrication

    Custom fabrication of steel towers and water storage tanks for reliable water storage and distribution systems.

    Solar Water Solutions

    Eco-friendly solar panel installation for water pumping systems, reducing energy costs and environmental impact.
